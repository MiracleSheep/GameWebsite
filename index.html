Space Dodge

What is this game?

Space dodge is basically a ripoff of flappy bird. I made it in order to practice using javascript.
Essentially, You are a rocket ship, and there are many pillars coming towards you. Your job is to dodge them. Goodluck!

How to play

  • Press the up arrow to go up.
  • You fall down naturally.
  • Dodge the obstacles.
  • If you hit the floor or ceiling, you die.
  • If you touch the pillars, you die.
  • That's it! Have fun!
